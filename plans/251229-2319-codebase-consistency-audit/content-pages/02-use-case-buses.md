## Designer Notes

### Bus-Specific Imagery
- Use school bus interiors (not coaches)
- Include students when appropriate (with model releases)
- Show driver perspective (dashboard view)
- Australian school bus colors (yellow/cream)

### Color Variations
- Primary: #2563eb (blue)
- Accent: #f59e0b (school bus amber)
- Alert: #dc2626 (red)
