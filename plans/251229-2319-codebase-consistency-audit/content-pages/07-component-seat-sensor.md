# The False Alert Problem

## Why Buckle-Only Systems Frustrate Drivers

A seat belt clicked into an empty seat looks exactly the same
as a seat belt clicked with a passenger sitting there.

**To the system? Identical.**
**To the driver? Constant false alarms.**

### The Real-World Scenario

**Passenger leaves bus for 5-minute stop**:
1. Passenger unbuckles, exits
2. Next passenger clicks empty belt (habit)
3. System alerts: "Seat 12 unbuckled!"
4. Driver checks: Seat is empty
5. Driver thinks: "Stupid system"

**Repeat this 10 times per trip. Driver stops trusting alerts.**
**That's when real safety incidents happen.**

---

## The Seat Sensor Solution

Two sensors. One intelligent system:

1. **Buckle sensor**: Detects if belt is clicked

## Capacitive Sensing Technology

How does a 1.5mm pad know someone is sitting there?

**Capacitive pressure detection**:
1. Sensor contains conductive fabric layers
2. Pressure (body weight) compresses layers
3. Electrical capacitance changes
4. System detects: *"Someone is here"*

# Installation: Under the Upholstery

## Why Hidden Installation?

The sensor goes **between the foam and the upholstery**:

1. **Protected from damage**: Passengers can't tamper with it
2. **Invisible**: Riders never know it's there
3. **Durable**: 10+ year lifespan, shielded from wear
4. **Effective**: Closest to passenger for accurate detection

---

## Installation Process

### First Seat: 15 Minutes

| Step | Time | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| Remove upholstery | 5 min | Unclip or unscrew seat cover |
| Place sensor | 1 min | Position on foam, connect to buckle sensor |
| Route wiring | 2 min | Tuck wire under foam |
| Reinstall upholstery | 5 min | Replace cover, secure clips |
| Test | 1 min | Sit in seat, verify detection |

**Additional seats**: 10 minutes each (upholstery removal mastered)

# Common Questions About Seat Sensors

### "Do I really need seat sensors? Can't I just use buckle sensors?"

You **can** use buckle sensors only. But here's the reality:

**Buckle-only systems**:
- 15-30% of alerts are false (empty clicked buckles)
- Driver alert fatigue develops
- Drivers start ignoring warnings
- Real unbuckled passengers get missed

**Buckle + seat sensors**:
- <1% false alerts
- Drivers trust every alert
- Immediate response to real problems
- Compliance maintained long-term

**The $65/sensor investment prevents years of alert fatigue.**
**Safety systems that drivers ignore are worse than no system at all.**

---

### "Will passengers feel the sensor?"

**No.** The sensor is 1.5mm thinâ€”thinner than a smartphone.
It's designed to be imperceptible:

- Between foam and upholstery (not under the passenger)
- Flexible (conforms to seat contours)
- No hard edges or bumps
- Temperature neutral (doesn't get hot/cold)

**Passengers sit. They stand. They never know.**

---

### "What about spills and accidents?"

**IP67-rated waterproof** when properly installed.

The sensor is sealed in TPU coating and protected by upholstery:
- Coffee spills? No problem
- Kids' juice boxes? No problem
- Rain from open windows? No problem
- Vomit? Clean upholstery, sensor is fine underneath

**10-year lifespan even in heavy commercial use.**

---

### "Can I retrofit this to existing systems?"

**Yes.** If you already have our buckle sensors installed:

1. Order seat sensors (match your quantity)
2. Remove seat upholstery (15 min/seat)
3. Connect seat sensor pigtail to buckle sensor
4. Reinstall upholstery
5. Display unit auto-detects seat sensors

**No system replacement. Just upgrade your detection accuracy.**

---

### "Do seat sensors work with child seats?"

**Yes.** Two scenarios:

**Child sitting directly on sensor**:
- Detects child (30kg minimum = ~3-year-old)
- Alerts if child unbuckles

**Child seat (car seat) on sensor**:
- Detects child seat weight
- Alerts if child seat belt unbuckles
- No false alerts from empty child seat

---

### "What's the warranty?"

**5-year full warranty** includes:
- Sensor replacement (any failure)
- Connector replacement
- Waterproof seal failure
- Pressure sensitivity drift

**Out of warranty?** Replacement sensors at current pricing.
Plug-and-play swap.
