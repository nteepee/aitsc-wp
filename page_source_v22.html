What are Passenger Monitoring Systems?

Passenger Monitoring Systems are a suite of integrated technologies designed to oversee and enhance the safety, security, and experience of passengers within a vehicle fleet. From public transport to school buses, these systems provide real-time data and alerts, ensuring accountability and immediate response capabilities.

They are crucial for modern fleet management, offering everything from high-definition surveillance to automated passenger counting and behavior analysis. AITS solutions provide unparalleled accuracy and reliability, creating a safer environment for everyone on board.
