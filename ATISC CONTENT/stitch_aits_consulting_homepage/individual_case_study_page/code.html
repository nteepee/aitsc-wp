The Client

Bus4x4 Group provides 4x4 conversion solutions for people movers, requiring robust safety systems for rugged environments.

The Challenge

Bus4x4 faced unique operational requirements regarding passenger safety. They needed a solution to ensure seatbelt compliance across all seats and simplify the monitoring process for fleet operators, while keeping installation and labor costs low.

The Solution

AITSC developed a custom technology solution specifically for Bus4x4. The system features:

  • Modular Design: To streamline part replacement so that only faulty components (like a specific sensor) need replacing rather than the whole system.
  • Plug-and-Play Architecture: To significantly reduce installation time and labor costs.
